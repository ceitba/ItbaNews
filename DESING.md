# Newsletter Design System

## Brand
University student newsletter for ITBA. Warm, human, editorial.
Reference aesthetic: university bulletin meets modern web mag — think
Substack meets a well-designed student paper, not a startup SaaS.

## Colors (CEITBA loosely)
- Primary: #1A3C6E (deep university blue)
- Accent: #F5A623 (warm amber — energy, highlight)
- Surface: #FAFAF8 (off-white, not pure white)
- Text primary: #1C1C1E
- Text secondary: #6B7280
- Border / divider: #E5E7EB

## Typography
- Display: Fraunces or Playfair Display (editorial, warm serif)
- Body: Source Sans 3 or Lato (readable, approachable)
- Mono (dates, tags, labels): JetBrains Mono
- Scale: 3× jumps — 14px body → 42px+ headings
- Weight contrast: 300 body vs 700/800 headings

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80px vertical desktop, 48px mobile
- Content max-width: 1120px centered
- Card gap: 24px
- Article line-height: 1.7

## Tone
- Student-first: approachable, never corporate
- Dates and events are first-class content
- No decorative emojis in UI chrome

## Anti-rules
- No purple gradients, no neon, no "startup slop" aesthetic
- No Inter or Roboto
- No centered hero-only layout (editorial flow, not landing page)
- No generic placeholder names (Acme, SmartFlow, etc.)
- No Unsplash placeholders — use abstract geometric fills or CSS art
- shadcn/ui allowed but all tokens (radius, color, shadow) must be overridden
